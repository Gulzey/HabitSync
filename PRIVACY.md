# HabitSync Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

HabitSync is a local-first habit tracker. The app is built to help you create habits, track daily completions, and view your progress over time.

## Data Stored By The App

HabitSync stores the following information on your device:

- habit names
- completed habit dates
- current streaks
- longest streaks
- the display name you enter in the app

This data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.

## Cloud Backup

Cloud backup is not active in the current release.

The app may show cloud backup as a coming soon feature, but habit data is not currently uploaded to cloud storage from the released app.

## Accounts

HabitSync does not currently require an account or login.

## Data Sharing

HabitSync does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

## Internet Connection

The current release is designed to work locally. Habit tracking data is stored on your device first.

## Data Deletion

You can delete habits inside the app. You can also remove all locally stored app data by uninstalling HabitSync or clearing the app storage from your device settings.

## Future Updates

Optional cloud backup may be added in a future release. If that happens, this privacy policy will be updated before the feature is made available.
